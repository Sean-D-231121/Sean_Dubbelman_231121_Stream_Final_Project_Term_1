Illenium

A Francisco native Nick Miller, better known as ILLENIUM is one of Denver’s most talked about EDM artists. ILLENIUM began making electronic dance music in early 2008 and quickly became known for his production quality and content. ILLENIUM’s latest EP titled, RISEN exemplifies just that. Miller teamed up with the internationally acclaimed Sirma for one of the songs on this EP, after hearing DropTalk’s remix of Wanderer and together they created the melodically beautiful track, Drop Our Hearts. This song was the first single from RISEN, premiering on Tuesday December 10th. Drop Our Hearts immediately rose to the #1 position on Dubstep.net.
